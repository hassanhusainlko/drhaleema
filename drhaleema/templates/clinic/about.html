Professional Overview

Integrating traditional healing with a patient-centric approach

Dr. Haleema Tul Husna is a dedicated Unani physician specialising in the holistic management of chronic and lifestyle-related conditions. With a BUMS qualification, she integrates traditional healing principles with a patient-centric approach to address the root causes of illness rather than merely suppressing symptoms.

She is particularly skilled in treating complex metabolic, respiratory, and musculoskeletal disorders through natural therapeutic interventions — drawing on centuries of Unani medical knowledge combined with a deep understanding of the individual patient's constitution and lifestyle.

Dr. Haleema's practice places equal emphasis on Women's Health, offering confidential and compassionate care for hormonal imbalances, reproductive concerns, and related conditions. Every patient receives a personalised treatment plan rooted in the Unani tradition of restoring balance and vitality from within.

What is Unani Medicine?

Unani (Greco-Arabic) medicine is a traditional system of healing with roots in the works of Hippocrates and Galen, refined over centuries in the Islamic world. It is built on the concept of four humours and uses natural remedies — including herbs, dietary regulation, and lifestyle adjustments — to restore the body's natural balance and strengthen its innate healing capacity.
